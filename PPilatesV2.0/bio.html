About

Ante & Post Natal Pilates taught by a physiotherapist trained in ante-natal pilates by the Australian Physiotherapy and Pilates Institute (APPI).

Pilates exercises are modified as your body and abilities change. Pilates is great at building core strength. If your abdominals, back, and pelvic floor/Kegel muscles are toned, they will support a more comfortable pregnancy and delivery.
Pilates is also famous for helping new moms get their figures back after baby is born!
